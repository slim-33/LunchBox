### Scanning Items
1. Open the app (starts on Scan tab)
2. Take a photo of fresh produce
3. AI analyzes freshness with **harsh grading**:
   - 90-100: Perfect
   - 75-89: Very good
   - 55-74: OK, use soon
   - 35-54: Poor, use now
   - 0-34: Don't eat
4. Item automatically saved to Fridge & Collection

### Managing Your Fridge
1. Tap the **Fridge** tab
2. See two categories:
   - **Fresh** (â‰¥3 days remaining) - Green badge
   - **Expiring Soon** (<3 days) - Orange badge
3. Each item shows:
   - Photo
   - Name
   - Days remaining
   - Storage tip
4. Tap trash icon to remove used items

### Generating Recipes
**Only appears when items are expiring soon!**

1. Go to Fridge tab
2. If items are expiring soon, you'll see "Get Recipe" button
3. Tap to generate a recipe using those ingredients
4. Recipe popup shows:
   - Recipe name
   - Ingredient list
   - Simple cooking steps
5. All recipes are 30 minutes or less!
